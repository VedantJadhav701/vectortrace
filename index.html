A New Paradigm in DevSecOps

VectorTrace integrates seamlessly into the development lifecycle.

Context-Aware Scanning

Our LLM analyzes code with a deep understanding of its logic and structure, identifying complex and zero-day vulnerabilities that pattern-based scanners miss.

Automated Mitigation

VectorTrace doesn't just find flaws—it generates precise, production-ready code patches and explains the reasoning, accelerating remediation cycles.

Proactive Threat Modeling

Describe a new feature in plain English, and our AI will generate a detailed threat model, helping you build security in from the start (Shift-Left Security).

Supply Chain Analysis

We analyze your entire dependency tree, identifying known CVEs and providing actionable intelligence on your open-source software supply chain.

Our Mission

To develop a state-of-the-art, LLM-based tool for the detection of vulnerabilities and malicious code within software, focusing on the open-source ecosystem. We are committed to not only identifying security flaws but also to generating automated, actionable mitigating measures to empower developers and secure the digital infrastructure we all depend on.

Our Vision

To create a future where secure coding is an intuitive and integrated part of the development process. We envision a world where AI-driven security tools work as collaborative partners with developers, making the open-source world safer, more resilient, and more innovative for everyone.
